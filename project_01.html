Cardo - Extra Condensed Typeface

Cardo is a distinctive, bold and extremely condensed aspect-ratio typeface that is perfect for eye-catching and impactful headlines. It's a typeface designed to mitigate the most common problems of ultra-condensed families, such as the huge discontinuity created by the white space of certain letters when placed side by side. Through careful design work, spacing and kerning, I was able to find a pleasing solution.
