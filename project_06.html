Slices of Life - Street Photography

The outside world constantly flows around us, and we are often in too much of a hurry to appreciate its diversity, its complexity... particularly the human one. We have a tiny moment each time, a frame to enter the life of a stranger, to imagine their story, their dreams, their concerns. The project is intended to provide a slow-paced look at all these slices of life that pass before our eyes so fuzzily.
