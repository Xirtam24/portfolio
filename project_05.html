Nexum - Gaming App

Nexum's app offers a revolutionary way to break into the world of video games. A series of parameters have been designed to categorise games according to their potential for personal growth and learning. With the help of highly specialised figures (such as psychologists and game designers), it is possible to exchange advice and information, as well as being able to create a customized path according to your goals.
