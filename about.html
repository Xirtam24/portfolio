Bio

I am Lorenzo Cortese, a young designer born and bred in the culture of industrial design because of my father, who has been doing this job for 30 years. His vision has greatly influenced the way I see the world and approach problems... every obstacle could always be a design problem. I currently study visual design and work as a 3D artist in the field of architecture.

Nevertheless I have several side passions: I love 3D, visual effects, cinematography and photography. I also have experience in product design and have even won an award for one of my projects (All Around Work award, sponsored by ADI). Regardless of the field of work, my goal is to design to the best of my ability and influence people's everyday lives through beauty.
