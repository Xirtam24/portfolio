Bio

My name is Lorenzo and I am a young designer born and bred in the culture of industrial design! I've been lucky enough to grow up around this job, as my father has been doing it for 30 years. I currently do visual design and work as a 3D artist in the field of architecture. I also have experience in product design and I even won an award sponsored by ADI for one of my projects!

But I also have other passions! I love videogames, cinematography and photography and lots of other stuff. I get hooked about everything and I absolutely have to read a few books about my new obsession. Regardless of the industry I work in, my goal is to design to the best of my ability and to influence people's daily lives in a positive way through clever design.
